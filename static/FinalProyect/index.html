Let's save our Planet

Global climate change already has effects that can be observed in the environment, which is causing a change in the life of our planet. Effects that scientists predicted in the past to emerge from global climate change are happening now: more intense heat waves, more frequent wildfires, longer periods of drought in some regions, glaciers have shrunk, ice in rivers and lakes are melting prematurely, sea level rises rapidly, plant and animal habitats have changed and trees bloom earlier.
This website enunciates the effects of climate change in order to awaken awareness in all of us about the need to take actions in favor of mitigating its effects on our Planet.
